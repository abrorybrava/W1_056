Knowing my #1 Bias! Winter Kim of aespa!

Posted on January 1, 2023 by Abrory Brava
aespa Karina Giselle Ningning

Kim Min-Jeong (김민정) well known as Kim Winter or Winter is dancer, singer, and my girlfriend that debuted in girlgroup called aespa. Winter was debuted with another 3 members (Karina, Giselle, and Ningning) on November 2020.

Well raised in Busan, South Korea. Minjeong-ie (her sweetie name) was born on January, 1st 2001. Caused born in winter season, SM Entertainment (her agency) gives her stage name 'Winter' what a cute name.

Like Karina and Ningning, Winter was scouted directly by a talent manager from SM Entertainment. She was scouted while at a dance festival. Although she initially had doubts about the offer, Winter later moved to Seoul with her family to become a trainee. After approximately four years of training, Winter finally debuted with aespa on November 17, 2020, with the single "Black Mamba."

Despite being considered rookies, aespa quickly became one of the fourth-generation idol groups to be reckoned with. Just a year after their debut, aespa had already won major awards, including Daesangs at various award shows such as the Asia Artist Awards and Melon Music Awards.

In 2021, the group also expanded its presence in the United States. aespa appeared on several programs, including The Kelly Clarkson Show, Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade, The Nick Cannon Show, and The Daily Show with Trevor Noah.

aespa also released their first mini-album titled "Savage" in October 2021. In addition to her group activities, Winter starred in the music video for the remake of "Free to Fly" by Kangta in the same year. She appeared in this music video alongside Sungchan from NCT.
